## Key Takeaways

1. **AI adoption starts small and practical**, not theoretical
2. **Security and trust** are top priorities before automation
3. **Simplify broken processes** before digitising them
4. **Critical thinking and communication skills** are rising in value
5. **The career ladder is shifting** from pyramid to diamond
6. **Advisory and assurance work** will define the future accountant

## Notable Quotes

> "If your workflows are messy, AI will multiply the mess."

> "Process before automation – control, simplify, then digitise."

> "AI supports people; people supervise AI."

> "Compliance is commoditised. Trust is the differentiator."

> "The accountant's job is shifting from preparer to protector."
